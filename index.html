For Clinics

Digitalize your operations and increase your revenue.

  • Create operation room schedules
  • Allow both your staff and freelance doctors to book rooms
  • Add patient records, notes, documents, and images
  • Accept online payments and generate invoices automatically
  • Get daily, weekly, and monthly financial reports

For Freelance Doctors

Work flexibly — manage professionally.

  • Discover available clinic rooms in your region
  • Check availability and book instantly online
  • Manage your own appointments, patients, and records
  • Get paid digitally and avoid cash transactions
  • Store journals, images, and files securely

No clinic of your own? No problem. Clinigo gives you everything to work like a pro.
